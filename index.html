RED FLAGS & AUTO-ADJUST

  • Sleep < 8.5 hr: Drop intensity 15%, add 20-30 min nap
  • Stress 5: Full recovery day, breathwork twice, easy walk + mobility only

TRAINING TERMS

  • RPE: Rate of Perceived Exertion. RPE 7 = 3 reps left. RPE 10 = max
  • CNS: Your power and speed system
  • Tempo 3-1-2: 3 seconds down, 1 second pause, 2 seconds up
  • Volume: Sets × Reps × Weight | Intensity: % of max

BODYWEIGHT CONTROL TO 325

  • Morning BW log daily. Aim −0.5 to −1.0 lb per week
  • If AM weight +5 lb: reduce dinner carbs by 1 cup and add 20-30 min easy walk
  • If AM weight −3+ lb overnight: add 1 cup rice at dinner and 600-800mg sodium that day

ELECTROLYTES AND SWEAT PLAN

  • Add 1-2g salt across meals on practice days. In heat or big sweat: 3-5g total
  • Sweat math: each 1 lb lost = ~16 oz fluid + 400-600mg sodium over next 2-4 hr
